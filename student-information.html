Student Information

Get the help you need, easily

We have a range of tutors who are wanting to help you in your academic journey. They will readily share their knowledge so that you can excel in your subjects.

Customize your search

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know my tutor will be awesome?

We screen all our tutors to make sure that they are high quality!

In order to become a tutor on Tutor4U, they must:
- Pass an interview
- Submit their CV
- Submit their academic transcript
- Go through police vetting

This ensures all tutors are checked before they are listed on our website. Additionally, every student who engages with a student can give a testimonial for that tutor.

Does it cost for students to use the Tutor4U website?

No- It's totally FREE!
The only cost to you is the rate to hire a tutor. All the tutors on our website dictate their own prices and every cent from the lessons go directly to the tutor.
