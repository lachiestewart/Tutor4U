how Tutor4U Works

For Students

Tutor4U makes it easy to find the tutor that suits every students needs and wants.
‍
Anyone can easily search through our database of tutors from around NZ. You can filter your search for subject, cost, location and gender so that you can find the perfect tutor for you!

Tutor4U wants to make this as easy as possible so that you can spend less time on finding the right tutor and more time learning!

For Tutors

Tutor4U helps tutors everyday to make connections with students who need their help.

We believe that tutoring is one of the best jobs that anyone can do. You can pass on your valuable knowledge to those that need it and have the autonomy to work on your terms.

Every tutor can create a personalised profile page on our website where students who are interested can message in!
