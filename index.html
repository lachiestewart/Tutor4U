Why Find a tutor on tutor4U?

Tutor4U allows you to easily find private tutors based on your needs so that you can find one as quickly as possible!

Additionally, all our tutors have been through police vetting when they register ensuring safety for all the community who want to engage with our tutors.

Step 1. Browse through the tutor4U tutors

Tutor4U has developed a state of the art filtering system so that you can quickly and easily find a tutor that suits your needs.

You can search based on:
- Subjects
- Tutor rate
- Teaching level
- Location
- Lesson format
- Gender
- Availability

Also, if you can't find a tutor to suit your needs, get in touch with us and we will do our best to find one for you.

Step 2. Read more on each tutor's Profile page

Each tutor has their own personalised profile page. Their pages will provide key details and more information so you can know a little about them before you get in touch.

Often they will mention their background, why they are qualified to tutor and their interests.

Step 3. Get in touch with the tutor

So what's the next step when you think you've found the right tutor? 

Each profile has a contact form to fill out that will allow you to email the tutor directly. They will then receive your details and reply to you so that you can organise a tutoring session.

Step 4. Get Tutored!

Now that you're in touch with the tutor, you can receive their academic services.

It's that simple!

Step 1. Create a free Tutor4U Login

So you want to join Tutor4U? That's fantastic!

The first step is to create a login. This will give you access to the Tutor's login portal so that you can start customising your profile and complete the steps needed to get published.

Step 2. Complete the onboarding process


Firstly, we will need to collect your profile details such as your contact details, the subjects you wish to teach & your profile photo.

Additionally, we will need you to complete police vetting. This is one of the ways we keep all the students and visitors to Tutor4U safe.

Lastly, you will need to pay for your annual subscription. This will allow you to access your own personalised tutor dashboard.

Step 3. Profile Approval

In order to gain access to your dashboard and be able to publish your profile, Tutor4U will need to read through your details to make sure it's appropriate for the platform and ensure all your police vetting documentation is completed correctly.

Once this is all done, you will have access to our Tutor Tribe communication channels, be able to edit your profile but best of all publish your profile & start receiving student enquiries!

Step 4. Publish your profile

Once all the above steps are taken care of there is only 1 last thing to do: Publish your profile!

Any visitors to the site will be able to view your profile and if they want to receive your tutoring services, they can easily complete the form on your profile page.

Step 5. Manage your student enquiries

Every time that someone completes the form on your profile page, you will receive the message directly to your email inbox. From there it's your responsibility to get back to them and manage the tutor-student relationship.

If you get too busy, you can always set your profile to the 'Not Available' status. This will cause the form to be taken down from your profile page. Then when you think you're ready to take students again, you can set your profile back to 'Available'!
