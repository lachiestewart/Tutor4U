2. Police Vetting

Great stuff!

The next step is to complete the paper work for Police Vetting. As you can appreciate, we want to take steps to ensure safety for all who engage with the platform. As part of our duty of care for students, we require all our registered tutors to undergo Police Vetting.

Please download and complete this form:
- Police Vetting Consent Document
Once completed, you will need to upload it through the online form below.

You will also need to upload 2 forms of ID (1 x Primary & 1 x Secondary)
Primary IDs:
- Passport (NZ or Overseas)
- NZ Firearms Licence
- NZ Full Birth Certificate
- NZ Citizenship Certificate
- NZ Refugee Travel Document
- NZ Certificate of Identity
Secondary IDs:
- NZ Driver Licence
- 18+ Card
- NZ Full Birth Certificate (Prior to 1998)
- Community Services Card
- SuperGold Card
- NZ Employee Photo ID Card
- NZ Student Photo ID Card
- Inland Revenue Number
- NZ Issued Utility Bill (Not older than 6 months)
- NZ Teachers Registration Certificate
- NZ Electoral Roll Record
- International Driving Permit
- Steps to Freedom Form
Note: Tutor4U won't submit the police vetting forms to the NZ Police until you have paid for a subscription. Also, the police can take up to 3 weeks to do their checks. During this time, your profile can be published but it will be displayed that your police vetting is pending.
