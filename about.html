Knowledge opens up opportunity.
Tutor4U was created to help kiwis with their academic struggles. We know how much time and effort goes into achieving in academics- so Tutor4U has tried to make it easier for all students to get the help they need!

Off the back of a very challenging Covid period for the events industry, NZ is craving the chance to come together and share some wicked experiences. As event professionals, together we can revamp the events industry and make it stronger than it has ever been before!

The goal is to improve the commerce, creativity and communities involved in all events across Aotearoa!

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."
- Nelson Mandela

About the Founder
Heya,

I'm Jason and I've been in tertiary education for around a decade. I've got a Bachelor of Science (Sport & Exercise Science) and now I'm doing a medical degree. I've been on the receiving end of tutoring and also had the pleasure of tutoring others for many years!

It's plain and simple: Academics can be challenging at times!
I know what a difference it makes having a quality tutor, teacher or mentor by your side and helping you along the way. They can take seemingly complex concepts and turn them into simple and understandable ideas that help you expand your knowledge.

Tutoring is one of those relationships where everyone benefits. Through the work that tutors do, kiwis can reach their academic potential and then go on to thrive on the world stage!

Stay awesome,
Jason Hart
About the Founder
Heya,

I'm Jason and I've had over a decade in the events industry dipping my fingers into many areas. I've organised some of NZ's biggest junior sports events with around 15,000 attendees, run business networking events and worked with music festivals right across the country. I love the problem-solving that happens in the lead-up to an event and the high-paced energy once it goes live!

I built EventPros.co.nz to help the NZ community connect with each other to create amazing events that bring many people together to have extraordinary experiences!
