Police Vetting

As you can appreciate, we want to take steps to ensure safety for of the community and all those who engage with Tutor4U. As part of our duty of care for students, we require all our registered tutors on the platform to undergo Police Vetting.

Please download and complete this form:
- Police Vetting Consent Document
Once filled out, you will need to upload the completed version through the online form above along with your Primary ID and Secondary ID.

You will also need to upload 2 forms of ID (1 x Primary & 1 x Secondary)
Primary IDs:
- Passport (NZ or Overseas)
- NZ Firearms Licence
- NZ Full Birth Certificate
- NZ Citizenship Certificate
- NZ Refugee Travel Document
- NZ Certificate of Identity

‍Secondary IDs:
- NZ Driver Licence
- 18+ Card
- NZ Full Birth Certificate (Prior to 1998)
- Community Services Card
- SuperGold Card
- NZ Employee Photo ID Card
- NZ Student Photo ID Card
- Inland Revenue Number
- NZ Issued Utility Bill (Not older than 6 months)
- NZ Teachers Registration Certificate
- NZ Electoral Roll Record
- International Driving Permit
- Steps to Freedom Form

Please note, even if you have completed the documentation, Tutor4U won't process it until you have paid your subscription. Also, the police can take up to 3 weeks to do their checks. During this time, your profile can be published but it will be displayed that your police vetting is pending.

More information about police vetting can be found on the NZ Police Vetting Service Website

What happens when the steps above are completed?

Once the above steps have been completed, you will be able to login and jump into your own personalised tutor dashboard. From there, you will be able to edit your profile, engage with the Tutor4U Tutor Tribe through our discord, monitor your billing and account settings and get updates on upcoming features to the platform.
‍
Our team will need to check your profile details to ensure that it's appropriate for the platform. This can take up to 2 business days but will only be required once. After it has all been checked off, you will be able to publish your profile page when you're ready and start getting students :)
