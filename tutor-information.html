Apply to be a Tutor!

Register your interest in becoming a tutor on the Tutor4U platform by clicking the button below and completing our application form. Once we've read over your application, we let you know further steps.
‍
Please be aware that you will need to submit your CV, academic transcript and also complete an interview before you will be invited to join our tutor community.

Attend an interview

Once you have sent your initial details to Tutor4U, you will need to schedule an interview so that we can get to know you a little better. This is also a great opportunity to ask any questions that you might have about the platform.

Profile approval

Once our team has sent you confirmation that you passed the interview, you will be invited to be a part of our tutor community! 
You will also be sent details about how to login to our tutor portal. These are the last steps to get you onboarded!

Managing your tutoring business

After that, you will be on your way to managing your own private tutoring business! At any time, you can adjust your profile description, availability and pricing. Any visitors coming to the Tutor4U website will be able to view your profile and get directly in touch with you if they require your services.
You will also have access to our exclusive Tutor4U Tutor Tribe Discord Community,  hear about any students needing services and be in the know about any of our future updates!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to be a tutor?

Firstly, complete our online application form.
‍
From there, you will be guided through the next steps. In order to get fully onboarded, all our tutors need to submit their academic transcripts, their CV and go through an interview.

The Tutor4U onboarding process ensures that all tutors on the platform are high-quality :)

What kind of grades do I need to tutor?

We require all tutors to have high academic grades and therefore we need to check academic transcripts when tutors apply.

We expect tutors to have the following grades in the subjects they wish to teach:

- NCEA Subject : Excellence grade in the subject

- University Paper : A or A+ in the paper

How much do tutors get paid?

Tutors decide their own rates!

Each tutor can decide on how much they charge themselves out at.

Our only rule with this is that tutors can't have a rate that is less than minimum wage.

How do tutors get students?

Every visitor can search for tutors in the database based on :

- Subject
- Subject Level
- Tutor Gender
- Tutor Rate
- Location
- Lesson Format
- Words on their profile page

That way, those who need tutoring can quickly and easily find a tutor that suits their needs. From there, they can complete the form on the tutor's pages to put in an enquiry. All submissions go directly to the tutor's email inbox so that they can reply.

What do I get when I join Tutor4U?

Once your application is approved you will:
- Be invited to join our exclusive Tutor4U Slack Workspace so that you can connect with the community, have access to resources, get invited to optional zoom meetings and stay up to date with updates to the platform.
- Have a personalised & customizable Tutor Profile Page that will be shown to all those who visit Tutor4U.
- Be able to manage your own workload and enquiries from students who may need you to teach them.

How much should I charge for tutoring?

You get to decide how much you charge- With lower rates, you may get hired more but you won't be charging as much.
What we recommend is that if you are new to tutoring, start with a lower rate and then as you get more students coming to you or you realise that you have limited time to teach everyone, then you can start increasing your rates.

Can I change my profile once it's made?

Yes - You can change your profile information at any time! 
As soon as you submit your details, a link is emailed to you that allows you to change the answers to the profile questions asked. Nothing is set in stone!

Does Tutor4U guarantee work to all the tutors?

No- getting work is the tutors responsibility.
However, we do our best to make it as easy as possible for tutors to advertise themselves to the students that may need them!
We recommend that tutors share their profiles on social media channels. This allows them to get more exposure and gives you a higher chance of getting hired :)

What are some ways I can get more students?

These are some ideas to get more students: Share your Tutor4U profile through the social media links, send a link of your profile to friends and family to see if they know anyone who would like your services, ask your old school if they would be happy for you to put up a couple of posters, get testimonials from your previous students (Note: These students must have been in touch with you through the Tutor4U website in order for this to work!)

Do I have to be a current university student or graduate to be a tutor on this platform?

No- but it's expected when you sign up that you have expertise and knowledge in the subjects that you wish to tutor.
